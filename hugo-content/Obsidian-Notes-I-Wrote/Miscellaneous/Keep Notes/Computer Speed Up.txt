[Registry Editor Tweaks]
--- EnableTcpFastOpen
> Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Edge
> TabDiscardingEnabled: 1
> EnableTcpFastOpen: 1

--- Remove popup for MS-Gamingoverlay
> HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\GameDVR 
> AppCaptureEnabled: 1

--- Disable Lock Screen Overlay
> HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\Personalization
> NoLockScreen: 1

--- Hark Disk Power Options Tweaks
> Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Power\PowerSettings\

> ...0012ee47-9041-4b5d-9b77-535fba8b1442

> Change the below Attributes to 2

----- Enable AHCI Link Power Management - HIPM/DIPM
> ...\0b2d69d7-a2a1-449c-9680-f91c70521c60 

----- Enable Secondary NVMe Idle Timeout
> ...\ d3d55efd-c1ff-424e-9dc3-441be7833010

----- Enable Primary NVMe Idle Timeout 
> ...\ d639518a-e56d-4345-8af2-b9f32fb26109

----- Enable AHCI Link Power Management - Adaptive
> ...\ dab60367-53fe-4fbc-825e-521d069d2456 

> Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Power Options > Change plan settings (of your active power plan) > Change advanced Power Settings > Hard disk > ACHI Link power Management and set it to Lowest. 
- If that is not an option, change it to HIPM+DIPM

--- Maximise Service Host Split Threshold
> Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control
>> SvcHostSplitThresholdInKB

--- Disable Throttle Network Bandwidth
> Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft
    >> Create Folder: Psched
        - NonBestEffortLimit: 0

> Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\PriorityControl

    - GPU Priority: 6
